FAQ

1. How do I book a tour or event?

Booking a tour or event is easy! Simply visit our website, choose your tour or event, and click the "Book Now" button.

2. Can I customize my travel itinerary?

Yes, you can customize your travel itinerary to suit your preferences. Contact us with your specific needs, and we'll help you plan the perfect trip.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal.

4. Can I cancel or change my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or change your booking. Please refer to our cancellation policy for more details.

5. Do I need a visa to visit Egypt?

Visa requirements vary depending on your country of origin. Please check with the Egyptian embassy or consulate in your country for the most up-to-date information.

6. What's the best time of year to visit Egypt?

The best time to visit Egypt is from October to April when the weather is cooler and more comfortable for sightseeing.

8. How do I become a partner with GOTO Egypt?

If you're interested in becoming a partner, please visit our partnership page for more information and to submit an application.
